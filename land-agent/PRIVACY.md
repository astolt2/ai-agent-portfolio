# LandAgent Privacy & Compliance Policy

**Effective Date**: March 2026  
**Version**: 1.0

This document is the official privacy and compliance policy for **LandAgent**, the public Custom GPT for U.S. land real estate investment scouting (Michigan-focused).

## Scope
LandAgent assists users with parcel research, filtering, ROI analysis, off-market owner identification, and cold-call letter drafting. All operations are strictly U.S.-only.

## Data Sources Used (All Public)
- County GIS and tax assessor records (public domain in every U.S. state)
- Public voter rolls (where legally available per state law)
- Public directory services (Whitepages-style)
- Uploaded knowledge files:
  - Michigan Assessors Manual
  - Land Due Diligence Checklist
  - Michigan Zoning Handbook
  - TCPA Rules
  - MI FOIA Handbook

## What Is NOT Collected or Stored
- No private emails, cell phones, or non-public contact data
- No paid people-search databases or restricted services
- No user conversation history is retained beyond the current ChatGPT session
- No data is sold, shared, or used for training

## Compliance Framework (U.S. Only)
- Fully compliant with federal **TCPA** (Telephone Consumer Protection Act)
- Compliant with Michigan **Telephone Solicitation Act** and **Home Solicitation Sales Act**
- DNC/TCPA registry checks are performed before any letter draft
- Public property records are exempt from Michigan privacy laws (per MI FOIA)
- GDPR does not apply (U.S.-only operations)
- No contact suggestions are generated for any individual on a Do-Not-Call list

## Owner Verification & Scoring
- Every off-market owner receives a public-record verification score (0–100)
- Cold-call letters are generated **only** for scores ≥70
- Unverified, deceased, corporate, PO Box, or low-score owners are automatically skipped

## Disclaimer in Every Output
All cold-call letters include this exact legal disclaimer:  
> “This communication is for informational purposes only and is not a solicitation if you are on any Do-Not-Call registry.”

## User Responsibility
You (the user) are solely responsible for:
- Verifying compliance in your own jurisdiction
- Obtaining any required consents before actual calls or mailings
- Following all federal, state, and local laws

LandAgent provides templates and public-record research only.
